Top 5 Horror Movies of 2025

1. Sinners

Directed by Ryan Coogler, this movie follows twin brothers who return to their hometown seeking a fresh start, only to confront an even greater evil. Metascore: 84.

2. Weapons

Directed by Zach Cregger, this film explores a community grappling with the mysterious disappearance of nearly an entire class of children. Metascore: 81.

3. 28 Years Later

Directed by Danny Boyle, this movie centers on survivors of a rage virus living on an island, and the horrors they encounter when they venture to the mainland. Metascore: 77.

4. Bring Her Back

Directed by Danny and Michael Philippou, a brother and sister uncover a terrifying ritual at their new foster mother's home. Metascore: 75.

5. Final Destination: Bloodlines

Directed by Zach Lipovsky and Adam B. Stein, a college student plagued by nightmares returns home to save her family from a horrific fate. Metascore: 73.
