Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show 2025

The Angels Take the Stage Again

Ever since I was young I was always in awe on how Victoria Secret angels looked, how big and astonishing those wings are, and how they carry themselves in a stadium full of people and celebrities. I've always dreamt of becoming one, I really want to become one lol. Anyways, here is my blog about the Victoria's Secret Fasion Show 2025.

After a year of transformation, the iconic Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show made a dazzling comeback in 2025 reimagined with a fresh, empowering vision. This year’s event blended timeless elegance with modern femininity, showcasing not only stunning lingerie but also artistry, inclusivity, and confidence.

The runway lit up with familiar faces and new stars, all embracing individuality and self-expression. The runway shimmered as models strutted with grace, while music performers, light, and dancers combined into a breathtaking showcase of beauty in every form.

Every angel shined in their own way. So did the performers of the show. But below are my favourite looks from this year's runway. I've also listed the artists and the songs they performed in this year's runway.

Behind the Glamour

Step behind the curtain and discover the artistry, hard work, and confidence that define the Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show. The Angels prepare not only their looks but also their mindsets—embracing empowerment and grace. The show’s soundtrack featured collaborations between top artists, adding energy to the ethereal runway. The creative direction this year embraced diversity and individuality. From the feathered wings to the satin robes, every detail told a story celebrating strength and softness side by side. Women of different backgrounds, culture, ethnicities, bodies, and skin color uniting as one to celebrate feminity and its uniqueness in every women. It is just not about flaunting bodies, big extravagant wings, and shiny and shimmering emblishments and undergarments. Its women empowerment.

Victoria’s Secret continues to evolve — honoring its heritage while championing modern femininity. The message is clear: every woman deserves to feel like an angel.
